Did you know?

  • There are approximately 195 to 500 dog breeds worldwide.
  • Puppies are born blind and deaf after 58-68 days of gestation.

  • From 3 to 12 weeks of age, puppies learn to

    interact with other dogs, humans, and their environment.

  • From 6 to 18 months of age, dogs undergo

    physical and behavioral changes as they reach sexual maturity.

  • From 6 years of age onwards, dogs enter their senior years

    and may show signs of aging, such as gray hairs, reduced activity, and health issues.

  • Dogs have an average lifespan of 10-13 years,

    depending on their breed, size, and health conditions.
